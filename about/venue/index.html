Venue

GoRuCo is hosted at the Google office in the Chelsea neighborhood of New York City. The address is 76 Ninth Avenue, between 15th and 16th Streets. The closest subway station is at Eighth Avenue and 14th Street, which has the A,C,E, and L lines. The Seventh Avenue and 14th Street station is only one long block further; the 1, 2, and 3 lines stop there.

Building security requires that all visitors present a photo ID.

If you are bicycling to the conference, you will be able to store your bicycle at the office during the day; contact us at info@goruco.com for details.
